A Short Break
Our Story

Tea, quick bites, and the tower next door

Tucked into a quiet corner of Müeyyetzade in Beyoğlu, Star Büfe has poured tea and turned out gözleme within sight of the Galata Tower since our doors first opened. Ours is the kind of place people duck into between errands and stay for a second glass of çay.

We keep the hours of the neighborhood around us — open for early risers and open for the last table of the night — so there's always time for a short break, whatever time it is.
